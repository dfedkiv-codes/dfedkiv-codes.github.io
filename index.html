03 — Projects

Research & Independent Work

ongoing MMP inhibition

MT1-MMP Selective Inhibitory Probes

Designing and synthesizing fluorogenic triple-helical peptide substrates that selectively report MT1-MMP (MMP-14) activity over MMP-13 and soluble MMPs. Uses Fmoc SPPS, RP-HPLC purification, MALDI-TOF characterization, and fluorescence kinetic assays to determine kcat/Km selectivity profiles.

Fields Lab · FAU

ongoing peptide synthesis

MMP-13 Fluorogenic Substrate Library

Building a library of triple-helical collagen-mimetic peptides to profile MMP-13 substrate preferences. Incorporates non-natural amino acids at P1–P3′ positions to establish selectivity determinants. Contributes to a manuscript in preparation.

Fields Lab · FAU

completed clinical data science

NHANES Cholesterol Epidemiology Analysis

Exploratory and inferential analysis of lipid panel data from NHANES 2017–2020 using Python. Examined demographic stratification of LDL/HDL/TG distributions, built linear regression models for cholesterol predictors, and produced publication-ready visualizations with seaborn + Plotly.

Independent · Data Science Curriculum

completed machine learning

Pima Indians Diabetes Classification

Binary classification project using logistic regression, random forest, and gradient boosting on the Pima Indians Diabetes dataset. Includes feature importance analysis, ROC/AUC evaluation, and cross-validation. Documented as a Jupyter notebook with clinical interpretation of model outputs.

Independent · Data Science Curriculum

in progress cheminformatics

MMP-13/OA Drug–Outcome Linkage Capstone

Capstone project integrating clinical epidemiology and cheminformatics: mapping MMP-13 inhibitor chemical space (ChEMBL) against osteoarthritis outcomes (SEER/TCGA) to identify mechanistic links between compound structure and clinical phenotype.
